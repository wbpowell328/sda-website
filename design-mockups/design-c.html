CASTLE Lab — Princeton ORFE

CASTLE Lab specializes in the study of sequential decision problems — settings that blend the flow of information and decisions over time, where each choice must be made knowing that its impact is uncertain. Our work spans energy, transportation, health, finance, and the foundations of AI, unified by a single modeling framework that organizes them all.
